Good questions

The things photographers ask.

Do I need to be technical?+
Not at all. You share one link with your client and we handle everything after that. If you ever want a hand, we're a message away.
Is it tasteful?+
Tasteful by default. Curation leans elegant and camera-ready, and you set the tone — from classic to bold — to match the way you shoot.
Who actually picks the pieces?+
A styling system composes complete looks from your client's own answers — her sizes, her comfort, her taste — guided by your studio's aesthetic. She always has the final say on what she buys.
Will my clients feel sold to?+
No. It feels like a gift — a personal stylist, on the house — not a store. She's never required to buy anything to enjoy it.
What does it cost me?+
It's free — for everyone, forever. Every photographer can style every client at no cost, with no catch. From there, optional Studio and Branded plans add more control and your own branding (see the three plans above). Founding studios get every paid feature free while we build together.
Can it match my brand?+
Yes. On branded plans we wrap the whole experience in your studio's look — and can build it straight from your website, the way a stylist would study your portfolio first.
